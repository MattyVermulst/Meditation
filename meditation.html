Reflection

Reflection is a meditation practise that invites you to ask yourself a question. Perhaps somthing such as "what are you most greatful for" Be aware of the feelings, not the thoughts, that arise when you focus on the question.

Mantra

Mantra meditation is a sacred utternace, a numinous sound, syllable, or a word. These words may be recited aloud, listened to or recited internally. This is to generate a vibration in our body to attract energy with.

Zazen

Zazen meditation is and ancient tradition that Buddhist's use that involves sitting upright and following the breath, particularly the way it moves in and out of body.
